About us

Mekedonia Homes is a non-profit charity founded in January 2010, dedicated to helping the elderly and those with mental disabilities who lack basic needs. We provide food, shelter, clothing, medical care, and support in a safe environment. Based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, with branches in several regions, we assist over 7,500 people across the country. Our team is committed to improving lives, and we invite you to join us through volunteering or donating.
