Donate to Mekedonia Charity Group

How donations are used?

Donations to Mekedonia Homes are used to provide essential services such as shelter, food, medical care, and clothing for the elderly and individuals with mental disabilities. They also support the staffing of caregivers and volunteers, helping us expand our services to reach more people in need across Ethiopia. Your contributions directly impact the well-being and quality of life of those we serve.
