Volunteer Opportunities

Mekedonia Homes offers volunteer opportunities to support our mission of helping the elderly and individuals with mental disabilities. Volunteers can assist in various roles, including providing care, helping with daily activities, fundraising, and administrative support. By volunteering, you can make a positive impact in the lives of those in need and contribute to our goal of creating a better, more inclusive community. If you’re interested, we welcome you to join our dedicated team and help us continue our important work.
